About Hezron

// builder. thinker. problem-solver.
Nairobi, Kenya

I'm Hezron Sande — a Moringa School graduate and full-stack developer who has spent the better part of four years quietly building things that matter. Not just websites — systems that solve real problems for real people across Kenya and beyond.

My approach blends sharp technical execution with a designer's eye for detail. Whether it's a fintech platform helping groups share subscription costs, an agricultural marketplace connecting farmers to buyers, or an environmental monitoring system built for governments and NGOs — I build with vision, clarity, and commercial sense.

I'm comfortable across the entire stack: React, Node.js, databases, deployment, APIs, and everything in between. I don't just write code — I think about user journeys, performance, and long-term maintainability.

When I'm not building, I'm studying what's next — automation, AI integrations, and how digital tools can accelerate growth for African businesses. I believe the next wave of powerful software will be built right here on the continent.

My Process

// how I turn your idea into a product
01
Discovery & Strategy
We begin with a structured conversation — understanding your goals, target audience, core functionality, and competitive landscape. I ask the uncomfortable questions early so the build is always pointed in the right direction.
02
Design & Prototyping
Before any code is written, I craft high-fidelity design mockups and interactive prototypes. You see exactly what you're getting — and you choose the direction that resonates. No surprises at delivery.
03
Build & Iterate
Development begins with a 50% upfront payment. I build in focused sprints with regular check-ins and demos. You give feedback at every stage — this is collaborative, not a black box. Speed without sacrificing quality.
04
QA & Launch
Every project undergoes rigorous testing across devices and browsers before going live. On delivery confirmation, the remaining 50% is settled. Your site launches clean, fast, and ready for real users.
05
Post-Launch Support
I don't disappear after delivery. A defined support window is included in every engagement — handling bugs, adjustments, and guidance as you settle into your new digital presence.
